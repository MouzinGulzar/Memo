## The Problem

Every growing business hits the same invisible ceiling.

The founder is the brain. The team is the memory. WhatsApp is the operating system. And it works — until it doesn't.

A customer calls back and whoever picks up has no idea who they are. A decision gets debated again because nobody remembers it was already made. A follow-up that was promised never happens because the person who promised it got busy. A new hire spends months asking questions that have been answered a hundred times. The founder can't step back because the entire business lives in their head.

This isn't a discipline problem. It's a structural one. Businesses generate enormous intelligence every day — and almost none of it gets captured anywhere. It lives in people's heads, in WhatsApp threads that scroll away, in voice notes nobody transcribes. And when those people leave, they take all of it with them.

The tools built to fix this made it worse. CRMs nobody updates. Task managers that become graveyards. AI assistants with no memory, no context, no ability to act. Every tool requires humans to feed it — and humans don't, because they're busy running the business.

**The average business runs on 6–12 disconnected tools and still loses most of its intelligence to human memory.**

What businesses actually need isn't more tools. It's a brain that captures everything automatically — and a staff that handles the operational work without being asked.

## The Solution

Memo is an AI-powered business operating system that lives inside WhatsApp.

You talk to it the way you talk to your best employee. Plain language. Voice notes. Quick texts between meetings. It doesn't just understand — it acts. It creates tasks, books appointments, stores memories, tracks relationships, and fires reminders autonomously. All of it, without you opening a single dashboard.

Every message goes through a cognitive pipeline that builds a complete picture before responding:

- Pulls your last 20 conversation exchanges for continuity
- Scans 30 open actions to know what's already in flight
- Runs semantic vector search across every memory ever stored — meaning-based, not keyword-based
- Checks your working session to know what you were just talking about
- Uses your replied-to message as the primary context anchor for precise resolution

All of that feeds into Gemini 2.5 Flash alongside your active skill configuration. One pass. Structured output. Actions executed, entities stored, memories written, response sent.

And it compounds. Every interaction makes the brain richer. Every entity mentioned — a customer, a vendor, a project, a decision — gets stored, linked, and made permanently retrievable. Six months in, your business has a brain that knows everything it's ever known. A year in, that brain is irreplaceable.

**The knowledge graph that builds itself:**

- `Entity` — every person, org, product, project ever mentioned, linked and searchable
- `Memory` — every insight, decision, fact — vector embeddings + importance scores
- `Relationship` — edges between entities, a live map of your business world
- `Action` — every task, reminder, appointment, follow-up — one universal store
- `CognitiveSession` — short-term working memory per phone, 1-hour TTL
- `ConversationEvent` — the full interaction timeline, forever
